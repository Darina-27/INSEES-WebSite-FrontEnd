ELECTRONICS AND INSTRUMENTATION SOCIETY, NIT SILCHAR

INSEES (Society of Instrumentation and Electronics Engineering), comprising of the faculty members and student and aims to play a pivotal role in the development of students as engineers by various out-of-curriculum and extra-curricular activities. The INSEES aims to be an active organization of the EIE department at NITS which promotes the interests of the members. Electronics and instrumentation engineering branch is open in 2009 in NIT Silchar. The branch offers eight semester (four year) B. Tech programme in Electronics and Instrumentation Engineering covering modern instrumentation, industrial instrumentation, electronics, computer applications, and software technologies.
